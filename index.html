Frequently Asked Questions

Quantum Cosmo School is the home and hope for hundreds of underprivileged children in the most remote region of Bangladesh. The children here are provided all the necessities including food, clothes, shelter, and education free of cost by Quantum Foundation. These children who, let alone education, could not find necessities met are now headed towards a promising future. In 2001, the school started its operation with only seven students now has 4,580 students in 2024. The students from the first batches are studying in top universities in Bangladesh.

To give a chance to the underprivileged children to develop their talent and unleash their potential.

The intention of Quantum Cosmo School & College.

  • To establish an opportunity for the education and self-development of the deprived children of various casts and creeds of the most deprived hilly region of the country.
  • To establish that it is possible to develop the potential, intelligence, and skills by using the power of meditation from a young age.
  • To improve the living condition of the tribal populations of the hill tracts so that they can contribute to the nation's progress.
  • Cultivate empathy and compassion among the ethnic groups
  • Help each student unleash their potential.

Our activities

Year 1999 marked the beginning of the Meditation Center of Quantum, “Quantumom”, in the Soroi Union of Lama Upazilla of Bandarban. While starting to work with a population, almost untouched by civilization, it was realized that age-old false beliefs, superstitions and ignorance added with orthodoxy, played a major role behind their underdevelopment. A survey found that, “Every additional year a child spends in school, adds 10-20% to his/her income.” Therefore, it is only by means of education that one can save himself from poverty, exploitation, ignorance and superstitions. The only way to recover them was by proper education.

Quantum believes that if provided with proper education, teachings of moral and humane qualities, these natives wouldn’t just shake off age-old ignorance and superstitions and grow into enlightened beings, but they would also work for the enlightenment, prosperity and self-reliance of their overall population. Quantum believes that every human deserves the right to develop his or her merit regardless of nationality, religion, cast or creed. Thus, it is written in history that all the great men in every nation and every generation, who revolutionized a society, had risen from the most deprived, underprivileged and underdeveloped segment of the society. Quantum Cosmo School presents us with the golden opportunity to materialize that very belief. So Build up Quantum Cosmo School & College.
